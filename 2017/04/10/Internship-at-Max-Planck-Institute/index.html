Internship at Max Planck Institute

I’m going to share my internship experience at Max Planck Institute during the period from 1st of April to the end of August in this blog. I, as an intern, being responsible of (if I can say so) IT development for MPIWG.

At the moment, my task is to build a “mirror website”. Why is it called mirror website? There’s a website currently hosted in Shanghai, China. Our team wants to build an exact same website, but hosting on our own server. That’s why they call it “mirror website”.

Instead of setting up the configurations directly on the server, I’m trying to use Docker, so that I can wrap everything into a container. The good part for this is, if we want to move the website to another server or environment next time, We can easily relocate it without setting all configurations again.
